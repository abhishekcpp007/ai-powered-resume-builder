Job Title: Full Stack Developer

About the Role
We are looking for a motivated Full Stack Developer to join our growing engineering team. You
will work across the stack — building responsive frontend interfaces and designing robust,
scalable backend services — to ship features our customers rely on every day.

Responsibilities
- Design, build, and maintain web applications using React/Next.js on the frontend and
  Python (FastAPI/Django/Flask) or Node.js on the backend.
- Write clean, well-tested, maintainable code and participate in code reviews.
- Design and consume RESTful APIs; work with relational databases such as PostgreSQL or MySQL.
- Collaborate with product and design to translate requirements into technical solutions.
- Containerize services with Docker and support CI/CD pipelines for reliable deployments.
- Monitor and troubleshoot production issues; contribute to observability and reliability.

Required Skills
- Proficiency in JavaScript/TypeScript and at least one modern frontend framework (React,
  Next.js, or Vue).
- Solid experience with Python and a backend framework (FastAPI, Django, or Flask).
- Experience with SQL databases (PostgreSQL/MySQL) and REST API design.
- Familiarity with Git, Docker, and CI/CD workflows.
- Understanding of cloud platforms (AWS, GCP, or Azure) is a plus.
- Strong problem-solving skills and ability to work in an agile, cross-functional team.

Nice to Have
- Experience with Kubernetes, GraphQL, or microservices architecture.
- Familiarity with automated testing (Jest, Pytest) and Test-Driven Development.
- Prior experience deploying applications to Vercel, Railway, or similar platforms.

Qualifications
- Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Engineering, or equivalent practical experience.
- 1+ years of professional software development experience (internships count).
